Step-by-Step Guide on How to Order

  1. Choose Your Items:

    Look through our menu and select the items you'd like to order.

  2. Order Button:

    On the same Page, there is a green coloured "ORDER HERE" button. Click on that..

  3. Fill The Form:

    Enter your name,contact number,select hostel and meal/item from drop-down list

  4. Enter the Quantity and payment Method:

    Enter Quantity and select whether you want to pay in cash or by UPI.

  5. Price Check:

    Click on "CHECK PRICE" button , you will get an alert indicated with the final bill amount.

  6. Delivery Charges:

    We charge 20 rs/- on every delivery or order..

  7. Payment:

    After receiving the order, pay the full amount to delivery partner by upi/cash...

Note: After submitting the form ,it's okay if you are not prompted to "Submitted successfully" or the details are not cleared from the form. After you click Submit button we have 100% recieved the order..
